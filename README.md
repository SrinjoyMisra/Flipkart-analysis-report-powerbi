## üìå Business Problem

Flipkart operates at a large scale with millions of orders, products, and customers. Business stakeholders require:

* A **centralized view** of overall performance
* Deep-dive analysis into **orders, customers, products, and profit of the company**
* The ability to identify **growth opportunities, inefficiencies, and risks**

This dashboard addresses these needs by transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights.
